Welcome to our scan stream page. This page displays information aboout the most recent scans from a certain event. Feel free to change the polling settings to whatever you like. Event selects the event displayed.
Timeout sets how often you check for new scans. Time passed sets the time frame (since now) in which you look for scans. So, for event 'event' we check every 'timeout' milliseconds for scans in the past 'time passed' seconds.
